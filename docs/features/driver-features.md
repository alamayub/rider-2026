# Driver Features

## Onboarding & Verification
- Driver account creation and role-based authentication
- KYC document upload and verification status tracking
- Vehicle profile and registration details
- City assignment and eligibility checks

## Availability & Dispatch
- Online/offline availability toggle
- Realtime location streaming to dispatch engine
- Incoming trip request card with timeout
- Accept/reject ride request controls
- Radius-based nearest-driver matching

## Trip Lifecycle Management
- Navigate to pickup flow
- Arrived-at-pickup status update
- Start trip and end trip actions
- Rider no-show and cancellation handling
- Trip event history tracking for auditability

## Earnings & Payouts
- Daily and weekly earnings summary
- Completed trip payout calculation
- Payment mode visibility (cash/online)
- Incentives and bonus tracking (phase expansion)

## Communication & Safety
- In-app rider contact options (masked where applicable)
- Incident reporting during active trip
- Safety escalation options for emergencies
- Account risk flags and temporary lock workflow
